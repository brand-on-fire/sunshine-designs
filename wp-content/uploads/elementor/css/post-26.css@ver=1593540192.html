Custom Printing

We sell you the Garments with your artwork printed on them. You pick the garments from our extensive catalog and send us your Artwork, which we will evaluate to make sure it will print correctly. If the artwork needs corrections, we will let you know what that is, so you can correct it. If you are unable to correct it, for a charge, we can do that for you. We will always tell you beforehand what that cost would be.

The best way to avoid art-charges is by following the artwork requirement found here. “Art Requirements”. If you don’t have artwork bring us your ideas and our in-house graphic design staff can create it for you. A Quote to produce artwork will be given after initial consultation about your vision for the project
