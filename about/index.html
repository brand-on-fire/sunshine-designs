About Sunshine Designs

Sunshine Designs was founded in 1996 and is based in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are a screen-printing company specializing in printing T-shirts, Sweatshirts, Canvas bags and the like. We operate 3 automated printing presses in a range of 12 to 18 colors. We are able to produce upwards of 10,000 shirts a day. In addition to high volume production we can make your Garment stand out above the crowd, incorporating any of the following special effects: Foil applications, HD inks, Puff, Suede, Discharge for soft hand etc. plus all other services that are needed in producing products for Retail stores and or unique needs of our customer, which includes but not limited to Folding, bagging, hang tagging, size stickers, blind shipping etc.
